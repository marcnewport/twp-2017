About

We work exclusively on web projects where the customer experience is paramount and with clients who understand the value of research-driven design.

Our client list includes Australian and state government departments and agencies, banks and financial organisations, arts and community organisations, fashion and accessory labels, art galleries, rock bands and start-ups.

This is what we do

Bespoke design

Our team of Melbourne designers will collaborate with you to build a world-class Shopify store. Clean, clear design that makes your brand shine and converts visitors into customers.

UX design

Our UX consultants will provide your company with insights and strategy that make sure your visual design is based on real data. Before you invest in a drastic change, we can prototype and test your ideas before comitting more resources.

UI design

Our visual and front-end designers will work with your brand to develop a “look and feel” for your store. Their work spans both visual and interaction design — adding subtle animations to level–up the shopping experience.

Front-end development

Our front–end developers live and breathe responsive, accessible and semantic code. They will transform a design concept into a robust, tactile digital experience that your customers are going to love.

Store setup

We have set up hundreds of Shopify stores over the years, and we know the interface inside–out. We will help you to get your store setup correctly the first time, and can educate you along the way so you are self–sufficient.

Theme customisation

One of Shopify’s strengths is the quality of pre–made themes available for sale in the Theme Store. We will help you to choose the right theme, and to make customisations to get it “just right”.

Design system architecture

Our hidden superpower is designing beautiful and pragmatic design systems for larger organisations. We will build a complete “digital design language” for your organisation, giving you the tools to rapidly build new web projects or pages without designer input.

E-commerce strategy

There’s a lot more to e–commerce success than just selling the right product. We will help you to make the right decisions at the right time about platform, product, marketing and more.

Replatforming and migration

If you’re not already selling on Shopify or Shopify Plus, we will help you to make the move with a minimum of fuss.

Launch management

There’s more to launching a new store than just pushing the button — we will help you to launch your new Shopify store with maximum velocity.
